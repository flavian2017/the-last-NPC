THE LAST NPC

Survive, Adapt, Conquer!
This game is based in a world where NPCs must learn to survive against all odds, from overcoming a zombie apocalypse to finding a safe haven. As you progress through the game, you will uncover the storyline, and maybe even uncover the secret behind the zombie apocalypse.
Designed purely for fictional purposes, this game immerses players in a narrative-driven adventure that tests their survival instincts and problem-solving skills. The characters, scenarios, and events are entirely fictional, crafted to provide an engaging and thrilling experience. Prepare to embark on a journey of survival, strategy, and hope in a world where NPCs must become heroes.
